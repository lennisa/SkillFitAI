# SkillFit AI

## Real-Time Multilingual Workforce Video Assessment Platform

SkillFit AI is an AI-powered workforce intelligence platform designed to make hiring more accessible, scalable, and trustworthy for multilingual and voice-first candidates. The platform combines multilingual speech interaction, behavioral video analysis, fraud detection, and workforce readiness evaluation into a unified AI-assisted hiring workflow.

The system enables candidates to participate in real-time AI video interviews using regional languages such as Hindi, Kannada, and English while allowing recruiters to evaluate communication quality, behavioral integrity, workforce readiness, and role alignment in a standardized manner.

---

# Problem Statement

Large-scale workforce hiring, especially in blue-collar and semi-skilled sectors, still faces several major challenges:

- Language barriers during interviews
- Low-literacy and voice-first candidate environments
- Lack of scalable remote verification systems
- Difficulty evaluating multilingual candidates fairly
- Fraud and impersonation during online interviews
- Inconsistent workforce assessment standards
- Time-consuming manual screening processes

Traditional hiring systems are often text-heavy, English-centric, and difficult to scale for diverse workforce environments.

SkillFit AI addresses these problems through a multilingual AI-driven video assessment platform that supports accessible, voice-first hiring workflows.

---

# Solution

SkillFit AI conducts multilingual AI-assisted video interviews where candidates can:

- Listen to interview questions in their selected language
- Respond naturally using voice input
- Be evaluated using speech intelligence and behavioral analysis
- Undergo integrity verification using webcam-based monitoring
- Receive standardized multilingual evaluation

The platform combines:

- Speech-to-text processing
- AI-assisted evaluation
- Video integrity monitoring
- Behavioral signal analysis
- Translation-based fairness pipelines
- Workforce readiness scoring

to create a scalable and inclusive hiring ecosystem.
